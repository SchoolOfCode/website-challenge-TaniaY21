All about me

I currently work as a customer service advisor for a Housing Association, which involves call handling, responding to emails, social media management and problem solving. I have been doing this for just over a year.

Why did you want to join the school of code?

I wanted a career change but did not know where to start. I was unsure about self teaching as I did not know if I was going in the right direction. I also wanted the support, and guidance, and wanted to develop the required workplace skills.

What do you hope to achieve by doing the school of code?

I hope to achieve the skills to take me into a career in front end technology. I want to learn how to study effectively and how to solve any technical coding problems using the correct resources. I would like to also achieve the confidence and connections to do this.

What do you do to manage time and learning schedule?

I make to-do lists and work through it each day. I tend to go back and forth as I learn better this way.
